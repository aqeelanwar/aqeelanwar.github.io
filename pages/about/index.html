About Me

Hi, I am Aqeel Anwar, a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at NVIDIA, where I work on advancing the perception systems that power autonomous vehicles. I hold a Ph.D. in ECE from Georgia Tech, where my research focused on building intelligent and efficient autonomous systems, particularly for resource-constrained environments.

Outside of engineering, I have a deep appreciation for architecture and design, especially skyscrapers. I enjoy building small-scale models and exploring urban spaces around the world.

I am also an avid traveler. Over the past decade, I have visited more than 20 countries and traveled extensively across the United States, gaining diverse cultural and professional experiences through international research, academic conferences, and personal exploration.

I occasionally write about machine learning, interview preparation, and my professional journey on Medium.
